Everything you need to know about our e-waste recycling program.

E-waste includes discarded electronic devices like smartphones, laptops, and batteries. Proper disposal prevents environmental damage.

EZ Waste is our innovative e-commerce platform that allows users to sell their old electronics and buy refurbished devices. Our goal is to make e-waste recycling accessible and sustainable for everyone.

We provide a seamless process where users can sell their old electronics through our platform. These devices are then either refurbished for resale or properly recycled to extract valuable materials, ensuring minimal environmental impact.

Over 50 million metric tons of e-waste are generated globally each year, with only about 20% being properly recycled. E-waste contains toxic substances like lead and mercury, which can harm the environment and human health if not disposed of correctly.

Recycling e-waste prevents toxic chemicals from polluting the environment and helps recover valuable materials like gold and copper.
