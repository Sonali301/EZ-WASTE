Welcome to Our Privacy Policy

At QuadCore E-Waste Management, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services.

We collect personal data such as name, email, phone number, and location to facilitate e-waste collection and recycling services. Additionally, we may gather device-related information for tracking and sustainability purposes.

We use collected data to improve waste management services, track recycling trends, and ensure compliance with environmental regulations. Your data helps us develop AI-driven solutions for efficient e-waste processing.

Your data is securely stored with encryption techniques to prevent unauthorized access. We ensure compliance with global data protection regulations.

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share it with government agencies, environmental organizations, and recycling partners to enhance waste management initiatives.

You have the right to access, modify, or request deletion of your data. If you have any concerns, feel free to contact us at privacy@quadcore-ewaste.com.
